An Inspiring man




After graduating college he went on to hunt to study abroad for his masters. Back then there was no internet, and without internet there was no way of researching colleges or getting applications. He mailed to several universities, hoping for a pre-application mail from at least one of them. At that time, it was very hard for Indians to study abroad in the US and obtain a Visa because of the difficult political relationship between the two countries. Only a full ride would grant you a visa. A couple of universities responded, offering him a full ride to graduate school.
